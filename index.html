Welcome to Ethiopia

Ethiopia invites you to discover why it is the origin of so much. As you explore this ancient land, you will be put in touch with your own origins— for this is the Land of Origins! From the magnificent rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the ancient obelisks of Aksum, Ethiopia offers a journey through time like no other.
